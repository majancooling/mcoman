About Majan Cooling

We started the company as an air conditioner service company in the year 2005. It began as a very small business only serving parts of Darsait and Ruwi in Oman. As years passed by, our company's business got more and more calls and now, we do our business almost all over Oman.

Our business is unique because we do the most premium job out there compared to other companies. We do not cut corners and only use the best brands for our services. We excel in our job with all our employees, working with utmost care and responsibility. And the best part is we always do this job with the most reasonable and affordable price.

Our Mission

The purpose of our business is to provide the best services to customers at a very affordable price.
